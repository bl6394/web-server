Database News and Update - August 31, 2019

We (thanks to Bjorn Loftis) have upgraded the ELP to another platform to be more efficient, while still maintaining the same interface. We have also added a set of Semantic Variables to the ELP. The specific citations for the semantic variables are below for each of these variables. We thank each of the following authors for developing such useful norms.

Citations for New Semantic Variables

Brysbaert, M., Warriner, A. B., & Kuperman, V. (2013). Concreteness ratings for 40 thousand generally known English word lemmas. Behavior Research Methods, 46, 904-911. (Concreteness)

Shaoul, C. & Westbury, C. (2010). Exploring lexical co-occurrence space using HiDEx. Behavior Research Methods, 42, 393-413. (ARC and Semantic Neighborhood Density)

Hoffman, P., Lambon Ralph, M. A., & Rogers, T. T. (2013). Semantic diversity: A measure of semantic ambiguity based on variability in the contextual usage of words. Behavior Research Methods, 45, 718-730. (Semantic Diversity)

Kuperman, V., Stadthagen-Gonzalez, H., & Brysbaert, M. (2012). Age-of-acquisition ratings for 30,000 English words. Behavior Research Methods, 44, 978-990. (Age of Acquisition)

Warriner, A. B., Kuperman, V., & Brysbaert, M. (2013). Norms of valence, arousal, and dominance for 13,915 English lemmas. Behavior Research Methods, 45, 1191-1207. (Emotion Variables)

DeDeyne, S.D., Navarro, D.J., Perfors, A., Brysbaert, M., Storms. (2018). The ?smallworld of words? English word association norms for over 12,000 cue words. Behavior Research Methods (in press). (Word Association Norms).

Pexman, P. M., Muraki, E. J., Sidhu, D. M., Siakaluk, P. D., & Yap, M. J. (2018). Quantifying sensorimotor experience: Body-object interaction ratings for more than 9,000 English words. Behavior Research Methods. (Body-Action Interaction Ratings)

In addition, over the years we often receive requests for the full behavioral dataset including the trial level data. Therefore, we (thanks to Melvin Yap) have provided links to two full sets of all trial level data, which reflects the full LDT and Naming datasets.
